Judy Bail

I use blogging for a variety of purposes, including curating my own work and thoughts process. The platform allows me to easily upload my writing and as well as my personal experience. It is simple to use and a platform that I use several times per week. It is also a non toxic healthy social platform where it is easy to pour out your heart, it is not only for writers! I've been using it for more than a decade now, lol.

My experience as a blogger has been incredible. I've reached my targeted audience, though I started off with very little followers. What a rollercoaster! I've used blogging as a venting space and suprisingly a lot of people have related to my personal experiences I think it also helps people who needs someone to listen. It is exactly as if you're journaling but people around the world can see it and understand it too. i think the best feature in blogging is that you can be anonymous. Have fun scribbling, toodles!
